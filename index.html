About me

A human depends on his environment but only he himself is albe to create it around him and for himself

I consider myself lucky, because my work coincides with my hobby. My education and many years of experience have helped me to develop a specific prioritization system in my work. First and foremost development starts with a conceptual design which must fit the architecture of the surrounding buildings, its practical uses and be in harmony with the rest of the area. Also scrutinous approach to the piculiarities of every plot of land (such as the type of soil, its depth, the humidity, whether there are any special landforms, the environment) and many other aspects keeping in mind the weather differences in our seasons. And last but not least is the attention to the wishes and preferences of the client. And of course the continious decorative view of the garden thoughout the year. To realize it all in practice I work with a team of professional gardeners, dendrologists (botanists specialized in bigger plants and trees), agriculteral technicians, irigation and water object experts.
